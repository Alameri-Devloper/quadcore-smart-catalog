## Hierarchy

Workspace
↓
Department
↓
Category
↓
Product Model
↓
Product

---

## Department

Represents the highest level of product organization.

Examples:

- Computers
- Security
- Networking

---

## Category

Groups similar products.

Examples:

Computers

- Laptops
- Desktops
- Monitors

---

## Product Model

Defines:

- Specifications
- Filters
- Display
- Comparison

Examples:

Gaming Laptop

Business Laptop

Workstation

---

## Product

Represents the actual item sold to customers.
